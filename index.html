About me

Hey there! I'm Mousemallow—though you can call me Murr if you'd like.

My name comes from a little piñata in Viva Piñata, a game I adored as a kid.

Mousemallow always felt like a burst of color and creativity—small but full of charm and possibilities, much like the art I create.


Through my comics, I love bringing vivid, colorful worlds to life—stories that pop off the page with a touch of whimsy and a lot of heart.
